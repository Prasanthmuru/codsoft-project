LED CONTROL USING ARDUINO BLUETOOTH INTERFACE

The rapid advancement of technology has ushered in an era of automation, simplifying our lives and freeing us from time-consuming tasks. This project focuses on designing a cost-effective, user-friendly, and easy-to-install using an Arduino Uno and Bluetooth module. This system, controllable through a user-friendly Android app, empowers users to manage various home appliances like fans, lights, air conditioners, and even automatic door locks from the comfort of their smartphones. Moreover, it offers enhanced security by allowing remote monitoring and control of your smart home, even when you're away.
